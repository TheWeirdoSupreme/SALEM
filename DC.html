SALEM DC Roster

Zatanna
Zatara is the daughter of the magician Giovanni "John" Zatara and Sindella, a member of the mystical Homo Magi race. Zatanna was raised by her father in a small house in New York, near the infamous Arkham Asylum. Zatanna's mother seemingly died after her birth, but she eventually learned that Sindella faked her death to return to her people.
Circe
Circe was a princess of Colchis in the Caucasus region. It was said that she was the daughter of the Sun himself and that her radiance was a gift to man. That her beauty had no parallel. She was wedded to the heir to one of the wealthiest kingdoms in the Caucasus, but he was a brute, drunk, and liar man, so she saw him as an animal, and finally killed him.
Dr. Fate
Kent Nelson was an archaeologist and, was exploring Mesopotamia when he discovered an underground pyramid. It contained Nabu, an ancient immortal who had been held in suspended animation for thousands of years. Kent released him, and Nabu decided to mentor the young boy in the secrets of the universe. He would serve as an agent of the Lords of Order in their never-ending battle against the Lords of Chaos.
Klarion The Witch Boy
Born in Limbo Town, where the former residents of Roanoke ended up after disappearing centuries ago, Klarion Bleak grew up in a town where necromancy was common and everyone had their own familiar. However, a spine-rider of the Sheeda arrived one day and transformed a monstrous form bonding them and their familiar together. They then attacked everyone in town, in the process, he was separated from Teekl, his familiar.
John Constantine
John Constantine was born in Liverpool, England. During his childhood experiments with magic, he attempted a spell taught to him that would make him a powerful magician. However, he was not told that the spell required a sacrifice; while performing it, his family home caught fire and resulted in the death of his mother and father.
Raven
Raven was born the half-breed daughter of a human named Angela Roth and the demon overlord Emperor Trigon. She was conceived when her mother joined a cult, which then summoned the demon lord Trigon, who raped her. Angela Roth, retreated to an alternate dimension where Raven was born and raised in Azarath. Growing up, Raven was taught to "control her emotions" by the Azarathian monks in order to suppress and control her inherited demonic powers.
